How We Work

Five steps. Nothing hidden.

The middle of a paint or drywall job is where most of the trouble starts. We've structured ours so the next step is always clear — to you and to us.

01

Walk & Quote

You request an estimate. We come walk it (or go on a video walkthrough) and send you a real number with scope.

02

Signed Scope

You sign the scope and a deposit. Now we're committed and so are you. No surprises after.

03

Schedule & Show

We schedule you, give you a window, show up when we said we would. You'll know when we're on the way.

04

Work & Document

Same crew start to finish. Photos before, during, after. You see progress without asking.

05

Walkthrough & Pay

Final walkthrough together. Punch list if needed, knocked out same day. Then we invoice and you pay by card.

Why Primero

A different kind of paint crew.

Most paint and drywall in the South is a flip of the coin. Either a giant GC who treats your house like a checklist, or a guy with a truck and no trail when something goes sideways. We sit deliberately in the middle.

The estimate is the price.

Honest scoping up front, no "discovery" upcharges mid-job. If something genuinely changes, we tell you before we touch it.

Photos for everything.

Before, during, after. We share them with you in real time. No more "did they actually do the trim" guesswork.

One crew, one foreman.

The people who quote your job are the people who do your job. No subcontracting handoffs. No "wait, who are you?"

English & Spanish, both fluent.
